Dr. Ventura-Cook

I have expertise in helping adults, adolescents, children and families understand difficulties affecting their relationships, career, academics and overall well-being. I apply interventions that alleviate your challenges. I am US educated, postdoctoral fellowship-trained, licensed psychologist who provides evaluations and counseling. I am a certified Comprehensive Behavior Intervention for Tourette's clinician. As a personal and executive coach, I collaborate with individuals explore sources of conflicts, and implement actions to diminish challenges in the workplace. I assist individuals find their motivations that lead to a meaningful life and feelings of well-being.

I use various diagnostic approaches including CBT to help clients understand conflicts, depression, anxiety, loss/grief, attention, emotional and behavior difficulties. I conduct ADHD evaluations of children and adults. I use CBIT techniques in treating Tourette's. I apply a multidisciplinary approach to treatment. I have several years of experience as a practitioner in the US that allows me to provide specialized service towards realization of your specific goals. My familiarity with different cultures, residency in several states, and fluency in Tagalog, Ilocano and English bring unique perspectives to my practice benefiting my clients.
