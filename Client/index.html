Dr. Ventura-Cook

I have expertise in helping adults, adolescents, children and families understand difficulties affecting their relationships, career, academics and overall well-being. I completed my Doctor of Philosophy in Psychology, internship, postdoctoral fellowship in the United States. I am a licensed psychologist who provides evaluations and counseling. Additionally, I am a certified clinician in Comprehensive Behavior Intervention for Tourette's Disorder (CBIT). As a personal and executive coach, I collaborate with individuals explore sources of conflicts, and apply actions to diminish personal and workplace challenges. Also, I assist individuals find their motivations towards a meaningful life and feelings of well-being.

I use a multidisciplinary approach to treatment including cognitive-behavior therapy (CBT), supportive therapy in understanding and alleviating clients’ conflicts, depression, anxiety, loss/grief, attention, emotional and behavior difficulties. I conduct ADHD evaluations of children and adults. I have several years of experience in the US and the Philippines as a practitioner that allows me to provide specialized service towards the realization of clients’ specific goals. My familiarity with different cultures, residency in several states, and fluency in Tagalog, Ilocano and English bring unique perspectives to my practice.
